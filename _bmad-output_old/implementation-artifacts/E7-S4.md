# Story 7.4: Build Conversation Detail Page

**Epic:** E7 - Messaging  
**Status:** ready-for-dev  
**Priority:** P1  
**Dependencies:** 7.1  
**Blocks:** None

## Story

As a user,
I want to view and continue a conversation,
so that I can communicate with my match.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I navigate to /messages/[matchId]
   **When** I am a participant
   **Then** I see the conversation history

2. **Given** the conversation view
   **When** I look at messages
   **Then** mine are on the right, theirs on the left
   **And** timestamps are shown

3. **Given** the conversation view
   **When** I type a message and send
   **Then** it appears in the conversation
   **And** the other person is notified

4. **Given** the conversation
   **When** I view it
   **Then** I see the match context (project name, score)
